Dressing: Balsamic vinegar and olive oil

Featured Pairing

Pecorino, Làmina - 2009

This dry, unoaked white wine, boasting aromas of honey, citrus, Mirabelle plums, and white flowers, is delicate enough to pair with this light summer salad. We love how the subtly tart Pecorino plays off the fresh tomato, and the wine’s flavors are friendly to the mild mozzarella and minty bite of basil. This would be extra-lovely if you splurged and used artisanal extra-virgin olive oil.
