Our Top 11
Spanish Wines
(...our lists go to 11)


11. Albariño, Burgans - 2007
Soft and round, with bright tropical fruit and a nutty minerality. 100% Albariño from Galicia's Rías Baixas zone, this aromatic white wine is named for a hill called Burgans, which overlooks the Atlantic.
#50834
Original Price: $14.99
Sale Price: $12.74

10. Txakolina, Ameztoi - 2007
Drinking this Basque wine is a lot easier than saying the names of the grapes in it: Hondarribi Zurri and Hondarribi Beltza. Dry and light-bodied with mineral-driven fruit and aromas of grapefruit and lemon, it is a perfect wine for seafood.
#12280
Original Price: $21.99
Sale Price: $18.69

9. Abadia la Arroyada Tinto Joven, Ribera del Duero - 2006
This unique little Ribera marries the juiciness of Beaujolais with the dark fruit of traditional Ribera. It's quite fruit-forward and totally unoaked, making it a nice quaffer on its own or with a simple platter of cheeses and charcuterie.
#20966
Original Price: $12.99
Sale Price: $11.04

8. Tres Picos Borsao - 2007 - 2006
Old-vine Garnacha shows brilliantly in this Tres Picos bottling. Red berries and cherries dance on the palate while earthy notes and balanced acidity keep things lively through the finish.
#98673
Original Price: $16.99
Sale Price: $14.44

7. Ribera Sacra "Viña Caneiro", D. Ventura - 2007
An elegant example of the Mencía grape that is reminiscent of a juicy cru Beaujolais. With grapey ease, lithe fruit, and great acidity, this cool-climate treat might change your perception of Spanish wines.
#21413
Original Price: $25.99
Sale Price: $22.09

6. Rueda Verdejo "Casamaro", Garciarevalo - 2008
A complex and exuberantly fruity white wine made from both young and extremely old Verdejo (145 years). On the nose, this wine offers an array of aromas - from peaches, to citrus, to tropical fruits - that carry through to the palate. Garciarevalo practices sustainable farming and makes this wine from 100% free-run juice, which is vinified in stainless steel to preserve its freshness. The wine has remarkable intensity while being crisp and clean: it's an excellent choice for people who love Sauvignon Blanc.
#21440
Original Price: $11.99
Sale Price: $10.19

5. Viña Mein, Ribeiro - 2007
Viña Mein comes to us from a tiny DO in northwestern Spain called Ribeiro, which means "riverside" in the Galician language. Many rivers crisscross this area, creating a cool, damp environment that is perfect for making clean, crisp whites. This one pairs quite well with seafood.
#71981
Original Price: $17.99
Sale Price: $15.29

4. Gravonia Rioja Blanco, Lopez de Heredia - 1998
This is the newly released vintage of Lopez de Heredia's "young" white wine. In the flush of youth, it is ready to drink, but of course shows the complexity of the slow, gentle aging process this house is famous for. Practicing Organic.
#11424
Original Price: $24.99
Sale Price: $21.24

3. AN/2 Anime Negra, Spanish Red - 2005
Tried a Mallorcan wine lately? This small-production red comes from 50- to 100-year-old vines of the indigenous varieties Callet, Mantonegro, Fogoneu, and Monastrell; the fruit is hand-picked and fermented with native yeasts. With an intriguing nose of bougainvillea and jasmine, and a lush mouthfeel with dark chocolate, coffee, and black plum flavors, this is a must-try.
#14553
Original Price: $23.99
Sale Price: $20.39

2. Viña Bosconia Reserva, Lopez de Heredia - 2000
Coming from the family-run and very traditional Lopez de Heredia estate, this wine has notes of strawberry, blood orange, pomegranate, coconut, and exotic spices. Viña Bosconia is produced from the juice of Tempranillo, Garnacha, Mazuelo, and Graciano, aged for three years in American oak barrels. The winery is one of the few in Rioja still owned by its founding family.
#14147
Original Price: $33.99
Sale Price: $28.89

1. Castillo Labastida Rioja, Reserva - 2001
Made from 100% Tempranillo grown on vines that are over 30 years old, this red is a blend of modern expertise and Old World charm. Aged in American barrique for 18 months, it shows warm, toasty vanilla notes blended with black cherry and cloves. Lively acidity makes it excellent with food, and it has a pleasant finish that sticks around forever.
#18443
Original Price: $14.99
Sale Price: $12.74
