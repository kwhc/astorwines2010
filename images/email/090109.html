Astor's Top California Syrahs & Zinfandels
...Our lists go to 11.


11. Zin 91 Old Vine Zinfandel - 2006
At age 91, American wine pioneer Hubert Opici decided to produce an old-vine Zinfandel to commemorate his 75 years in the wine business. Bold and rich with layers of black fruit and spice.
#21465
Original Price: $14.99
Sale Price: $12.74

10. Copain Syrah, "Tous Ensemble" Mendocino County - 2007
A dark and inky Syrah from a much-loved Sonoma producer. This bottle has pleasant hints of chalk amidst a mouthful of dark, ripe fruit. Though the wine may see the inside of a barrel, it is refreshingly free of oak flavors.
#21270
Original Price: $22.99
Sale Price: $19.54

9. Qupé Syrah, Central Coast - 2007
A tasty and aromatic Syrah from Qupé, a specialist in Rhône grapes. This medium-bodied wine boasts classic Syrah flavors: black fruit and a touch of black pepper.
#87624
Original Price: $16.99
Sale Price: $14.99

8. Bogle Zinfandel California - 2007
#64044
Original Price: $11.99
Sale Price: $10.19

7. Sobon Estate Zinfandel, Hillside - 2008
This delicious Zinfandel is produced from old vines in Amador County. Flavors of cocoa and blackberries ooze from the glass, making this sturdy and bold wine an absolute joy with hard cheeses or a grilled steak.
#16976
Original Price: $9.99
Sale Price: $8.49

6. Ravenswood Zinfandel, Vintners Blend - 2007
This is Ravenswood's introductory Zinfandel. A full-bodied red with black berry fruit, cherries, and pepper aromas. If you like this Zin, try the rest of their wines: you'll immediately see the family resemblance.
#64744
Original Price: $9.99
Sale Price: $8.49

5. Quivira Zinfandel, Dry Creek - 2006
Quivira is a small, organic, family-run estate in the heart of Sonoma in California. This Zinfandel is produced with fruit from several different vineyards throughout the Dry Creek Valley. It's a dry red wine with an abundance of red raspberry fruit with underlying layers of tobacco, cedar, and mocha.
#15225
Original Price: $19.99
Sale Price: $16.99

4. McManis Family Syrah, River Junction - 2007
Soft and fleshy, with ripe tannins, complex flavors of blackberry and cherry, and just a touch of cedar and smoke. This red screams for black pepper-crusted pork loin.
#10990
Original Price: $9.99
Sale Price: $8.49

3. Biale "Black Chicken" Zinfandel - 2007
The Biale family, immigrants from Northern Italy, began growing grapes in Napa in the 1930s – Zinfandel only. This is a spicy, rich Zinfandel with lots of ripe, dark fruits on the palate.
#20208
Original Price: $45.99
Sale Price: $39.09

2. Neyers Zinfandel "Pato Vineyard" - 2006
A rich and chocolatey Zin that shows great balance and enticing texture. The vines are 110 years old and produce fruit of complexity and concentration. Additionally, the grapes are left on the vine 30 days longer than the average harvest date in this vintage, making for a superb finished product.
#21543
Original Price: $17.99
Sale Price: $15.29

1. Dashe "l'Enfant Terrible", Zinfandel - 2008
What puts this Zin in a class all its own, apart from its low alcohol and solid tannic structure, is that it is fermented using natural yeasts rather than commercial ones. This gives it all the character of its place of origin: the Alexander Valley. On the nose, it's full of sweet mixed red and black berries, but the palate is totally dry. A real treat from the Zin category.
#20797
Original Price: $25.99
Sale Price: $22.09
