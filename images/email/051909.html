Astor's Top Sakés
(our lists go up to 11)


11. Kira Honjozo Saké (720)
Nutty and rich on the palate, this is a saké that tastes great when it's heated up.

10. Rin Draft Saké, Nama (500)
This is one of the richest, most decadent nama sakés we've tasted. Round in texture, it is full of buttery, creamy aromas and finishes very well.
#18762
Original Price: $16.99
Sale Price: $14.44

9. Kamoizumi Komekome Saké (500)
Off-dry (meaning lightly sweet). This is the ideal warm weather saké that's fruit forward with aromas of honeydew melon. Drink chilled and enjoy as an apéritif before lunch or dinner.
#15505
Original Price: $21.99
Sale Price: $18.69

8. Sawanotsuru Zuicho Saké, Junmai (1.8)
This is a richer, fuller-styled Junmai Daiginjo, with flavors of steamed rice that offer sweetness on the palate. This is a nice easy-drinking choice for parties. Only serve chilled.
#91333
Original Price: $43.99
Sale Price: $37.39

7. Dassai 50 Saké, Junmai Ginjo Nigori (720)
There are not many beverages that sound right when you describe them as ''lightly cloudy.'' This phrase is right on, though, if you're speaking of unfiltered saké also known as nigori saké. This is rich, milky, and full of green apples and mixed melons on the palate.
#17089
Original Price: $26.99
Sale Price: $22.94

6. Kosui Shizejo Saké, Junmai (180)
A very citrus-driven cup saké that's full of ripe white grapefruits and tangy green apples. Rich in style and delicious drunk straight from the fridge.
#21211
Original Price: $5.99
Sale Price: $5.09

5. Ichinokura Saké, Junmai Nama Genshu Nigori Tokubetsu (750)
Quite a mouthful of saké with many elements contributing to this supremely complex seasonal spring release brew that has just arrived. Milky on the palate with aromas of enoki mushrooms, earthy broth, lemons and limes. Mildly sparkling and dry but not bone dry. Drink chilled alongside equally earthy dumplings.
#21343
Original Price: $33.99
Sale Price: $28.89

4. Otokoyama Saké, Junmai (720)
One of the most popular sakés, this very dry (SMV +10) bottle from Hokkaido features grain-based flavors and vivid acidity. This full-bodied saké is a traditional favorite with izakaya fare and beef-based dishes such as yaki-niku.
#59694
Original Price: $26.99
Sale Price: $22.94

3. Dassai 50 Saké, Junmai Ginjo - NV (720)
This is a very soft and subtle Ginjo with aromas of fresh apples and peaches on the palate. This is relatively dry on the palate and makes an excellent match to steamed fish or any number of salty, light appetizers.
#20392
Original Price: $26.99
Sale Price: $22.94

2. Ozeki Dry Saké, Junmai (750)
A solid, dry saké that you can warm up during the cooler months of the year or drink chilled during the warmer months.
