Astor's Top California Merlots
...Our lists go to 11.


11. Markham Merlot Napa - 2005
Ripe fruit and a weightless feel in the mouth set this Merlot apart. It's from a great Napa vintage and made in a friendly style that allows its red cherry flavors to shine brilliantly. A spectacular Merlot that will help you out in just about any red wine situation.
#88804
Original Price: $21.99
Sale Price: $18.69

10. J Lohr Merlot, Paso Robles - 2006
The aroma is dominated by blueberry and cherry fruit and layered with spicy, cedary oak on the finish. A medium-bodied Merlot that can be enjoyed alone or alongside any number of meat dishes.
#83005
Original Price: $13.99
Sale Price: $11.89

9. Sterling Merlot Napa - 2005
Aged in small oak barrels, this Merlot is the definition of an approachable red wine. The oak imparts subtle spicy vanilla notes on the nose, while plenty of dark fruit flavors keep the palate happy. This red goes with almost anything.
#66344
Original Price: $21.99
Sale Price: $19.99

8. Bogle Merlot - 2007
Bogle’s Merlot is another great example of the soft and silky wine that this varietal can be. Concentrated aromas of earthy spice and cherries surround the senses. Mere hints of pipe tobacco and spicy oak lend depth to the finish, reinforced by firm, tannins.
#99825
Original Price: $9.99
Sale Price: $7.99

7. Hahn Monterey Merlot - 2006
An aromatic Merlot with spiced plum aromas and accessible tannins. The acidity is nicely balanced and adds brightness to this dry red wine.
#84844
Original Price: $11.99
Sale Price: $9.99

6. Ch. St. Jean Merlot Sonoma - 2005
Almond essence, dark fruits, and lingering toast on the finish will make this an instant favorite. This is a phenomenal value in top-flight California Merlot.
#63714
Original Price: $24.99
Sale Price: $21.24

5. Michael Sullberg Merlot Reserve - 2007
Tremendous California Merlot under $10! Packed with lush black cherry and vanilla flavors, this smooth and satisfying Merlot delivers what so many others promise.
#65031
Original Price: $9.99
Sale Price: $8.49

4. Estancia Merlot, Central Coast - 2006
Big, dark, chocolatey fruit flavors characterize this wine from Estancia, one of California's most consistent wineries. This shows that unmistakable Merlot mouthfeel, followed by a long, oak-kissed finish.
#66804
Original Price: $13.99
Sale Price: $11.89

3. Cakebread Merlot, Napa - 2005
Rich aromas of blackberries, plums, black tea, and vanilla dominate the palate of this Napa Valley Merlot. The oak aging adds a spicy element on the finish.
#86694
Original Price: $63.99
Sale Price: $54.39

2. Sinskey Merlot, Carneros - 2005
Full of exotic fennel-laced plum flavors and aromas of violets, this is a refreshing Merlot that really stands out from the rest.
#64445
Original Price: $22.99
Sale Price: $19.54

1. Green Lion Merlot, Napa Valley - 2006
The Green Lion is Chris Ringland's new California project. Known for making award-winning wines in Australia, he's now decided to try his luck in California: so far, the results are quite promising. This Merlot packs a punch of perfectly ripe fruit and brambly spice.
#19861
Original Price: $15.99
Sale Price: $13.59
