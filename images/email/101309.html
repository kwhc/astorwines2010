Astor's Top 11 California White Wines
...Our lists go up to 11.


11. Pacific Rim Riesling - NV
This Riesling was clearly made with assertively spiced food in mind. Lots of soft, tropical fruit flavors lead to a clean, dry finish.
#62154
Original Price: $9.99
Sale Price: $8.49

10. Amala Chardonnay - 2007
An easy-drinking white wine that's medium-bodied with aromas of orchard fruit and baked apples. Chill and enjoy.
#22142
Original Price: $8.99
Sale Price: $6.99

9. Cline Viognier - 2008
A delightfully crisp wine that is bursting with tropical fruits and spices. It’s brilliant as an apéritif, but its freshness will also brighten up just about any meal you can think of.
#17623
Original Price: $10.99
Sale Price: $9.34

8. Elizabeth Spencer Sauvignon Blanc - 2008
This alluring Sauvignon Blanc comes from the winemaking team of Elizabeth Pressler and Spencer Graham. Thirty-year-old vines and extended lees contact give it a rich mouthfeel, with grapefruit and white flower aromas bursting from the glass.
#18446
Original Price: $16.99
Sale Price: $14.99

7. Kenneth Volk Malvasia - 2006
This Malvasia comes from Monterey. Because of its rarity, Kenneth Volk considers this one of his heirloom varieties. The wine shows great acidity, with bright floral notes along with hints of citrus and melon. Delicious!
#19869
Original Price: $19.99
Sale Price: $16.99

6. Vina Robles Chardonnay, Monterey - 2008
This winery was born of a dream in the mid-'90s by two Swiss entrepreneurs who fell in love with the Central Coast of California. The fruit for their Chardonnay was sourced from Monterey, a very cool microclimate. Aromas of apple and honeyed citrus lead to flavors of baking spices, tropical fruit, and a warm toasty finish. An oaky style of Chardonnay.
#15294
Original Price: $18.99
Sale Price: $16.14

5. Westerly Sauvignon Blanc, Santa Ynez Valley - 2007
Westerly Vineyards sustainably farms 85 acres of low-yielding grapevines on a 200-acre ranch located in Happy Canyon. This Sauvignon Blanc shows beautiful concentration with notes of honeysuckle, sweet-and-sour citrus, and grass notes.
#96997
Original Price: $19.99
Sale Price: $16.99

4. Heitz Cellar Chardonnay - 2007
In operation since the early 1960s, Heitz Cellar remains a family-owned, family-operated winery. Heitz recently revamped the flavor profile of its Chardonnay by reducing the amount of new oak it sees, allowing the fruit's fresh acidity to shine through.
#13614
Original Price: $24.99
Sale Price: $21.24

3. Melville Estate Chardonnay - 2007
Melville is owned by father and son Ron and Chad Melville, with Greg Brewer as their winemaker. After owning several vineyards in Napa, Ron Melville came to Santa Rita and never looked back. The microclimate of the Santa Rita Hills appellation, which is characterized by cool temperatures, prominent coastal winds, and foggy mornings, is well suited to growing the grapes of Burgundy. This Chardonnay offers aromas of toast, white peaches, and grapefruit. It is refreshingly bright and carries a lengthy finish.
#12400
Original Price: $28.99
Sale Price: $24.64

2. Quivira Sauvignon Blanc, Fig Tree Vineyard - 2008
A beautifully aromatic Sauvignon Blanc that shows aromas of grapefruit and key limes. This is a refreshing white wine with a very dry finish.
#20273
Original Price: $16.99
Sale Price: $14.44

1. Arcadian Chardonnay, Sleepy Hollow Young Vines - 2005
Joe Davis is a master of California winemaking. He studied in Burgundy, adopting many Old World techniques, and later brought them to his efforts in Santa Barbara, California. This Chardonnay will remind you of a white Burgundy, but it shows more exuberant notes of citrus, white flowers, and stony minerals on the finish. A sophisticated California Chardonnay.
#18421
Original Price: $33.99
Sale Price: $28.89
