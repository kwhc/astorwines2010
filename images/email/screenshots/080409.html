Astor's Top South African Wines
...Our lists go to 11.


11. Brampton Shiraz - 2006
A solid Shiraz from South Africa. The Brampton wines from the Stellenbosch region are made in a style that boasts upfront fruit on the palate. This is unoaked and shows all the red, peppery fruit you've come to love in this versatile grape.
#14014
Original Price: $13.99
Sale Price: $11.89

10. Oracle Pinotage - 2008
An usual and ripe style of Pinotage with complex notes of black pepper, smoke, tomato, and dark fruit on the nose. The palate is packed with sweet mixed berry flavors. An unoaked and crowd-pleasing red wine.
#21622
Original Price: $6.99
Sale Price: $5.94

9. Onyx Cabernet Sauvignon - 2003
A dark and brambly Cabernet that effortlessly mingles Old and New world styles. The nose is full of jammy cassis notes, while the palate is quite structured. This wine sees 14 months in new French oak, which imparts subtle yet undeniable spicy notes.
#21298
Original Price: $15.99
Sale Price: $13.59

8. The William, Graham Beck - 2003
A delicious and complex blend of Pinotage and Cabernet Sauvignon. This dark red shows lots of spicy oak and cassis on the nose and palate.
#15193
Original Price: $14.99
Sale Price: $12.74

7. Cape Indaba Chenin Blanc - 2008
One of Astor's best white wine values, this Chenin Blanc has tantalizing aromas of ripe pears and flowers, with exuberant tropical fruit on the palate. This is great with cheeses, fish, and light pasta dishes.
#70010
Original Price: $5.99
Sale Price: $5.09

6. Ken Forrester "Petit" Chenin Blanc - 2008
Anyone who loves New Zealand Sauvignon Blanc needs to taste this very quaffable Chenin Blanc from South Africa. It's herbal and ripe, with the slightest touch of residual sugar. Perhaps the best part is that it's not a wine you have to think about too much: it tastes great and it's easy to drink.
#20467
Original Price: $9.99
Sale Price: $8.49

5. Excelsior Chardonnay - 2007
A medium-bodied Chardonnay with a pale golden color, fruity aromas, and floral honey notes to round out the apple and lemon-lime flavors. With its creamy texture, this would be perfect with a dish like shrimp étouffée.
#11086
Original Price: $7.99
Sale Price: $6.79

4. Steenberg Sauvignon Blanc - 2008
Made from a Loire Valley clone, this South African Sauvignon Blanc tastes of tropical fruit and citrus and has slight grassy notes. Dry and crisp with loads of minerality and a long finish, Steenberg's fresh white wine is great with shrimp cocktail.
#11629
Original Price: $8.99
Sale Price: $7.64

3. Mulderbosch Sauvignon Blanc, Stellenbosch - 2008
An absolutely beautiful, world-class Sauvignon Blanc. It's very crisp with layers of delicious ripe melon and mineral flavors. Serve with a simple fish dish and let this wine shine.
#77184
Original Price: $19.99
Sale Price: $16.99

2. Mulderbosch Rosé - 2009
A South African rosé based on Cabernet Sauvignon. This wine is held back a year for aging, ultimately developing deeper and more complex berry aromas. It's ready to drink now.
#77223
Original Price: $10.99
Sale Price: $9.34

1. Oracle Sauvignon Blanc - 2008
Lately we're finding that South African wine is nearly unbeatable when you're looking for value and top quality at all levels. Drink this crisp white as an apéritif wine to whet your appetite, or enjoy it throughout a seafood dinner. It's unoaked, refreshing on the palate, and driven by citrus fruit flavors.
#11626
Original Price: $6.99
Sale Price: $5.94
