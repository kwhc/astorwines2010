Astor's Top 11 Ports & Madeiras
...Our lists go up to 11.


11. Churchill's White Port (750)
White Port seems like such a contradiction. This is still the very lovely fortified wine that you've learned to love, it's just made in a different fashion and therefore turns a different color. This a great wine with hard cheeses, walnuts and more. Some people enjoy this just slightly chilled.
#57712

Sale Price: $13.59

10. Fonseca Vintage Porto - 2000 (750)
The Fonseca port house was founded in the early 1800s. This vintage port is incredibly approachable. The fruit is dark, ripe and full of mullberries. The tannins are surprising accessible today.
#56106

Sale Price: $80.74

8. New York Malmsey Madeira, Barbeito - NV (750)
Recreating the style of Malmsey Madeira that was all the rage in New York, oh, about two hundred years ago. This is part of a special historic series newly developed by The Rare Wine Company. Nutty aromas: almonds, hazelnuts and exemplary acidity.
#10950

7. Justino's Fine Rich, Madeira - NV (750)
Dark amber color, clean nutty aromas, light complexity. Medium bodied with a clean, enjoyable finish.

6. Broadbent Madeira 10 Yr. Malmsey - NV (750)
This dark, amber, coffee-scented Madeira will bring a smile to your face after a meal. With tangy dark fruits and plenty of woodsy character, it shows all the caramel complexity we like in these special fortified wines.
#21232

Sale Price: $39.09

5. Colheita Madeira, Broadbent - 1996 (750)
Smooth with a nice, full texture and nutty finish. "Colheita" Ports and Madeiras are required to spend 7 and 5 years respectively in oak before release. This example is pefect as a dessert with Manchego and quince paste.
#21233
Original Price: $41.99
Sale Price: $35.69

4. Quinta do Infantado Ruby Port - NV (750)
Elegant and rich, this dark, juicy port has excellent balance of fruit, acid, sugar, and tannin. Not too sweet, not too big, it is all-around easy to drink and enjoy.
#57855

3. Barbeito Malvazia Madeira - 1954 (750)
This vintage bottling of Malvazia, also known as Malmsey, offers seemless notes of thyme, toasted almonds, lemon and lime aromas. The acidity, fruit and sweetness are finely tuned with perfect balance.
#11474
Original Price: $249.99
Sale Price: $212.49

2. Quinta do Infantado Vintage Port - 2003 (750)
This is from the quirky vintage of the millennium, we dare say. It's fleshy with dark berried fruit, peppercorns, and dark chocolate. This house style is on the more reserved side of the spectrum when it comes to the level of sweetness, with superb balance and a mineral tannic finish.
#57534
Original Price: $49.99
Sale Price: $42.49

1. Charleston Sercial Madeira, The Rare Wine Company - NV (750)
Charleston Sercial celebrates the refined, delicate Madeiras that the 18th- and 19th-century Charlestonians prized. Like any great Sercial, this miraculously balances power and delicacy. With its nutty, tangy flavors, it is not only a wonderful apéritif, it's also great with many foods.
#12277
