7. Ch. Haut Brisey, Médoc Cru Bourgeois - 2005
Look out Cabernet lovers! Here is a big mouthful of dark, pulpy, voluptuous fruit. The tannins are ripe and indicative of a successful vintage and, while, the wine sees some wood, the nose and palate aren't oaky. Perfect both for left bank lovers and Napa Cab fans.
