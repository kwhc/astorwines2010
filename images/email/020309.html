Our Top 11
New York Wines
(...our lists go up to 11)


11. Pellegrini East End Chardonnay - 2006 (750 mL)
With 70% fermented in stainless steel tanks, this cool-climate North Fork Chardonnay has fresh pear and citrus notes. It’s a crisp and lovely alternative to California Chardonnay.
#66384
Original Price: $10.99
Sale Price: $9.34

10. Dr. Konstantin Frank Rkatsiteli - 2006 (750 mL)
Full of grassy, grapefruit-driven fruit with spicy notes, strawberries, and a bit of white pepper. Long and graceful, this Finger Lakes wine finishes very well.
#66333
Original Price: $19.99
Sale Price: $17.99

9. Macari "Sette" - NV (750 mL)
The terroir of the North Fork is reputed to be quite like the terroir of St. Émilion and Pomerol, making the region an appropriate home for Cabernet Franc and Merlot. Spice and plum prevail in this artfully integrated Bordeaux blend.
#20355
Original Price: $14.99
Sale Price: $12.74

8. Channing Daughters, Rosso Fresco - 2006 (750 mL)
This Long Island wine has a translucent light red color with purple overtones. It is dry, medium-bodied, balanced, and juicy, showing plum, black cherry, black raspberry, Asian spices, a touch of smoke, and a slight meaty/gamey note.
#18943
Original Price: $17.99
Sale Price: $15.29

7. Channing Daughters "Scuttlehole" Chardonnay - 2007 (750 mL)
This dry, fruit-driven Chardonnay really shows its cooler-climate Long Island roots. Pair this with all the classic matches like salmon, lobster and chicken and even fresh cheeses, vegetables and raw fish.
#67009
Original Price: $15.99
Sale Price: $13.59

6. Wolffer, Cabernet Franc - 2005 (750 mL)
Cabernet Franc, known to flourish in the Loire Valley, also thrives on Long Island. Enjoy this bottle's spicy black fruit and relish its relative ease in contrast to many of its Loire Valley brethren.
#13404
Original Price: $21.99
Sale Price: $18.69

5. Dr. Konstantin Frank Dry Riesling, Finger Lakes - 2007 (750 mL)
Riesling does very well in the Finger Lakes, and this one is no exception. A beautiful nose of honey and peaches is followed by pear, lemon, and minerality on the palate. The fruit and acidity are in perfect balance, and the finish is silky smooth.
#63123
Original Price: $18.99
Sale Price: $16.14

4. Lenz Chardonnay "White Label", North Fork - 2005(750 mL)
One of our favorite North Fork winemakers, Lenz's Eric Fry always bottles a crisp, flavorful Chardonnay under the "White Label." The mix of steel tank and oak fermentation keeps the wine lively while adding a touch of spice.
#67224
Original Price: $10.99
Sale Price: $9.34

3. Pellegrini Merlot East End Select, North Fork - 2003 (750 mL)
This is a smooth, easy-drinking Merlot with inviting peppery and raspberry notes. The North Fork of Long Island is becoming famous for its accomplished expressions of Bordeaux grapes: taste this one and you'll be a doubter no more.
#66394
Original Price: $11.99
Sale Price: $10.19

2. Millbrook, Tocai Friuliano - 2007 (750 mL)
This is an off-dry white wine from the Hudson Valley region of New York. The grape has its origins in northern Italy and found its way to New York State decades ago. Full of yellow fruits and white pepper on the palate, this is slightly sweet and a great match for spicy foods.
#18959
Original Price: $14.99
Sale Price: $12.74

1. Paumanok Vineyards "Tutthuls Lane" Cabernet Sauvignon - 2005 (750 mL)
A full-bodied, structured North Fork red wine that's full of cassis, blackberries, and spice. The type of wine to open up when you're having a great steak.
#20039
Original Price: $59.99
Sale Price: $50.99
