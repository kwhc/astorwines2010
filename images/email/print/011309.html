Our Top 11
Wines from the Loire Valley
(...our lists go up to 11)


11. Muscadet, Guy Bossard - 2006 (750 mL)
This Muscadet from Guy Bossard has all the telltale signs of honest winemaking. It's a mineral-driven white with distinct citrus fruit and fresh white flowers. Organic since 1975. Fully Biodynamic as of 1996.
#12220
Original Price: $14.99
Sale Price: $12.74

10. Sancerre "Mélodie," Gaudry - 2006 (750 mL)
Take a sip of Gaudry's brilliant Sancerre and you'll immediately taste the impact that 50-year-old vines can have on a wine. This is well concentrated, showing white fruit, fresh citrus, and floral aromas. Quite simply a gorgeous bottle of wine, and an excellent example of what old vines can do in the right hands.
#17694
Original Price: $23.99
Sale Price: $20.39

9. Chinon Vieilles Vignes, Grosbois - 2006 (750 mL)
The production of this old vines Cabernet Franc are miniscule so get your hands on a bottle of this while you can. On the palate full of dark, concentrated berries. Plenty of perfect ripeness and clay minerals to finish this voluptuous red wine. Changes in the glass by the minute.
#17765
Original Price: $24.99
Sale Price: $21.24

8. Pouilly-Fumé "Prestige", Dom. du Bouchot - 2006(750 mL)
Pouilly-Fumés come from the Loire Valley and are made exclusively from the Sauvignon Blanc grape variety. This is a dry white wine that's full of white flowers and grapefruit aromas. Bright with flavors and fresh acidity making it a prime match for seafood.
#17696
Original Price: $21.99
Sale Price: $18.69

7. Vouvray Demi Sec "Clos Naudin" - 2005 (750 mL)
Mildly sweet with aromas of acacia and honey on the palate.
#19294
Original Price: $38.99
Sale Price: $33.14

6. Savennières "Jalousie", Dom. du Closel - 2006 (750 mL)
You'll certainly feel a bit jealous of anyone drinking a glass of this if you're empty-handed. This is stellar Chenin Blanc, dry, with aromas of pear skin and honeysuckle. Best enjoyed just slightly chilled.
#17117
Original Price: $22.99
Sale Price: $19.54

5. Sancerre "Apud Sariacum", Raimbault - 2007 (750 mL)
Like all great examples of Sancerre, this wine is characterized by a perfect marriage of place and grape variety. It shows perfectly ripened fruit and a fine-toned, racy minerality of white limestone.
#23133
Original Price: $23.99
Sale Price: $20.39

4. Anjou Rouge "Les Copines Aussi", Sablonettes - 2006 (750 mL)
A light and fruity red wine made from the Gamay grape variety. It's light-bodied with red cherry and currant fruit on the palate. From a Natural winemaker.
#11415
Original Price: $14.99
Sale Price: $12.74

3. Vouvray "Cuvée Tradition", Pinon - 2007 (750 mL)
A gentle sweetness in the attack brings on flavors of apple and quince, accented with spice, leading to a pleasant citrus finish with resonance and length.
#22105
Original Price: $22.99
Sale Price: $19.54

2. Muscadet, Dom. de la Pepière - 2007 (750 mL)
Great, refeshing Muscadet with vibrant minerality. Of course, this is perfect for oysters, but with its good round body, it's also great with light poultry or vegetable dishes. From a Practicing Organic estate.
#21384
Original Price: $12.99
Sale Price: $11.04

1. "Adonis" La Grapperie - 2005(750 mL)
Here at Astor we love to discover new wines made from Pineau D'Aunis. The combintation of black peppercorn spice and ripe, juicy fruit make this bottle real winner. Have it with food and/or make sure to decant to taste it at its best. This less than 5-hectare estate belongs to Renaud Guettier, who purchased his first parcel of vines in 2004. His vineyards are divided into 15 separate plots, each with a unique microclimate. He also plants Chenin, Grolleau, Gamay, and Côt.
#20731
Original Price: $27.99
Sale Price: $23.79
