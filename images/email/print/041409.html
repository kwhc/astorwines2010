Our Top 11
Chilean Wines
(...our lists go to 11)


11. Manta Sauvignon Blanc - 2008
A light, easy-drinking white wine made even brighter by fresh melon and lemon zest flavors. This is a purely refreshing wine that is a hit at parties, so consider picking up a case.
#12655
Original Price: $6.99
Sale Price: $5.94

10. Echeverria Cabernet Sauvignon - 2007
From the Curico Valley of Chile comes this rich Cabernet Sauvignon. Pretty blackberry and red cherry fruits greet the nose, and there is a touch of mint on the finish.
#12218
Original Price: $8.99
Sale Price: $7.64

9. Terra Andina Merlot - 2007
Full of plump plum fruits and a bit of spice, this Merlot is a great red wine to open up with friends (or even enemies - it's that good).
#15425
Original Price: $7.99
Sale Price: $6.79

8. Los Vascos Cabernet Sauvignon - 2007
A straightforward Cabernet Sauvignon from the Chilean property of Domaine Lafite Rothschild. It's extremely approachable, with ripe cassis fruit and soft tannins to please anyone's palate.
#54434
Original Price: $9.99
Sale Price: $8.49

7. Casa Lapostolle Merlot "Cuvée Alexandre" - 2006
This rich and lovely expression of Merlot is produced in Chile. Plummy fruit is wrapped in a velvety-smooth texture that only the best Merlots exhibit. Enjoy this with beef stews and braises.
#58154
Original Price: $21.99
Sale Price: $18.69

6. Amayna, Sauvignon Blanc, Leyda Valley - 2007
Leyda, a sub-region of San Antonio, is located in a valley between the Coastal Mountain Range and the Pacific Ocean, running parallel to Chile's Central Valley. This wine has lots of tropical fruit, dominated by white pineapple and white flowers. A dry, distinctively New World Sauvignon Blanc.
#19470
Original Price: $19.99
Sale Price: $16.99

5. Terra Noble Gran Reserva Carmenère - 2007
The Carmenère grape came to Chile via Bordeaux in the late nineteenth century, and lately it's been more at home in South America than France. The red wines made from this grape can range from refreshing and easy-drinking to full-bodied and dense. This is one of the brawnier ones, with rich, dark fruit and a touch of spiciness from oak aging.
#17595
Original Price: $14.99
Sale Price: $12.74

4. Montes Alpha Chardonnay - 2006
Beautiful Chardonnay character comes through in this Chilean white that can compete with California Chard any day. Find tropical fruit and butterscotch on the palate with a long, round finish. .
#10576
Original Price: $21.99
Sale Price: $18.69

3. "Neyen" Espiritu de Apalta, Colchagua - 2004
Neyen means "spirit" in the native Mapudungun language. The Neyen vineyard was the first in Apalta (Colchagua valley); these Carmenère and Cabernet Sauvignon vines were planted in 1890. This red wine is a full-bodied and full of cassis and blackberry spiced fruit.
#19484
Original Price: $56.99
Sale Price: $48.44

2. Echeverria Unwooded Chardonnay - 2008
A Chardonnay unlike any other in the New World. This is a hand-crafted wine without any trace of oak influence. Aromas of tropical fruits and pears intermingle with mouth-watering acidity, while the wine retains a lovely round feel on the palate.
#12611
Original Price: $9.99
Sale Price: $8.49

1. Amayna, Pinot Noir - 2006
Pinot Noir has come a long way since it was first planted in Chile, and we were extremely impressed with the Amayna bottling. Taste it blind and there can be no doubt that this is pure, delicious Pinot Noir - albeit a bit heftier in style than most. It's full of wild raspberries, dark cherries, and spice.
#19478
Original Price: $28.99
Sale Price: $24.64
