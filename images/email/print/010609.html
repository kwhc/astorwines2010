Our Top 11
Wines from Argentina
(...our lists go up to 11)


11. Kaleido Bonarda, Familia Mayol - 2006 (750 mL)
Bonarda is one of the most popular and successful red grapes grown in Argentina. This fantastic wine is from the Mendoza region and gives us everything we want in a full-bodied red: spice, lush black fruit (blueberries and blackberries), and medium grip from the tannins.
#14421
Original Price: $13.99
Sale Price: $11.89

10. Don Nicanor, Chardonnay-Viognier - 2007 (750 mL)
This white wine comes from Mendoza, Argentina, high up in the mountains where the climate is cooler - meaning that the wines turn out fresher. A blend of Chardonnay and Viognier, it is full of white fruit and vanilla bean flavors from oak aging.
#18130
Original Price: $14.99
Sale Price: $12.74

9. Catena Chardonnay - 2006 (750 mL)
Argentina's great international success, Catena sets high standards for their wines. In fact, they've repeatedly skipped vintages when they felt the wines have been anything short of excellent.
#50008
Original Price: $16.99
Sale Price: $14.44

8. Bodega NQN, Malma Cabernet Sauvignon - 2005 (750 mL)
Just like all the great grapes of Bordeaux, Cabernet Sauvignon is made into terrific, accessible, modern wines in Argentina. The "Malma" bottling is no exception: big cassis fruit and eucalyptus are the show-stealers here.
#19778
Original Price: $12.99
Sale Price: $11.04

7. Pie de Palo, Viognier - 2008 (750 mL)
This dry, unoaked white wine is full of delicate aromas of ripe pineapple and citrus fruits. A versatile white wine, it makes a fascinating companion to simple poultry or seafood dishes.
#18439
Original Price: $7.99
Sale Price: $6.79

6. Viña Alicia Petit Verdot "Cuarzo", Lujan de Cuyo - 2005 (750 mL)
Petit Verdot is one of those little-known grape varieties that originated in the Bordeaux region of France and made its way to the brilliant winemakers of South America, where it flourished. This exemplary bottling is rich, full-bodied, and spicy, with red currants and cherries on the palate.
#19477
Original Price: $53.99
Sale Price: $45.89

5. Yellow and Blue Torrontés - 2008 (LTR)
What a delicious way to be environmentally conscious! This floral, lychee-flavored white wine is crisp, clean, and makes a perfect apéritif; also, it will stay remarkably fresh in your fridge over several days.
#21070
Original Price: $11.99
Sale Price: $10.19

4. Crios Cabernet Sauvignon - 2006 (750 mL)
A rich red wine from Susana Balbo, one of the greatest producers in Mendoza. Full of blackcurrants, spice and cherries on the palate, this is vibrant throughout and long on the finish.
#19044
Original Price: $14.99
Sale Price: $12.74

3. Yellow and Blue Malbec - 2007 (LTR)
This environmentally friendly Mendozan Malbec is delightfully free of oak and full of fresh and vibrant acidity. The nose is reminiscent of dark berries and the tannins are pleasingly present. Take a tetra home tonight and discover what all the fuss is about!
#20568
Original Price: $11.99
Sale Price: $10.19

2. Benmarco Malbec - 2006 (750 mL)
Susana Balbo makes this supple and full-bodied red from 50-year-old vines. The age of the vines gives this wine uncommon richness and concentration, making it an extraordinarily sophisticated Malbec.
#75487
Original Price: $18.99
Sale Price: $16.14

We just can’t resist this Malbec: It’s a perfect example of how well this lovely red grape can do in Argentina. Made on a Practicing Organic estate in the Mendoza region, this full-bodied wine shows dark berry fruit and a spicy edge on the finish.
