Our Top 11
New Zealand Wines
(...our lists go to 11)


11. Sileni Sauvignon Blanc - 2007
This New Zealand Sauvignon Blanc offers everything we look for in a fresh white from this neck of the woods. It is lightly herbaceous, with upfront gooseberry fruit, juicy acidity, and perfect ripeness. While this is a dry wine, it is absolutely packed to the brim with fruit.
#12522
Original Price: $17.99
Sale Price: $15.29

10. Kim Crawford Pinot Noir - 2007
Flavors of cherry and wild strawberry, silky tannins, and a light kiss of oak all hang in perfect balance here, and the finish lingers. Another in a string of tremendous efforts from one of the most talented Pinot Noir producers in New Zealand.
#11152
Original Price: $17.99
Sale Price: $15.29

9. Seresin Chardonnay - 2006
The cool temperatures in Marlborough help maintain lovely acidity and pure fruit character in this Chardonnay, producing a wine of true complexity and depth. These Chardonnay grapes are hand-harvested and vinified using natural yeasts. The winery uses 20% new oak, aging the wine for thirteen months in barrel. In the end the wine is both smoky and citrus-laden, with lots of ripe fruit on the nose and palate.
#20589
Original Price: $21.99
Sale Price: $18.69

8. Delta Vineyards, Pinot Noir - 2007
We can't get enough of this New Zealand Pinot Noir. It's like drinking a glass of pure heaven with all the freshly crushed raspberry fruits. This is a light-bodied, dry red wine that's just amazing alone but works wonderfully with meat dishes as well.
#17821
Original Price: $18.99
Sale Price: $16.14

7. Huia Sauvignon Blanc - 2007
Gooseberry, tropical fruit, and lime notes abound in this textbook New Zealand Sauvignon Blanc. A classic wine calls for a classic pairing, so try it with shellfish for a phenomenal experience.
#12240
Original Price: $16.99
Sale Price: $14.44

6. New Harbor Sauvignon Blanc - 2007
Lots of grapefruit and honeysuckle on the nose. Well balanced with bright, crisp acidity.
#17625
Original Price: $10.99
Sale Price: $9.34

5. Villa Maria Sauvignon Blanc - 2008
A great example of New Zealand Sauvignon Blanc with typical ripe tropical-citrus fruit, zippy acidity, and smoky, peppery flavors. Enjoy this with well-seasoned chicken, pork, or fish dishes, especially if they're a little spicy.
#10140
Original Price: $12.99
Sale Price: $11.04

4. Cloudy Bay Pinot Noir - 2006
With plums, dark fruits, exotic spice, and toasty oak, this sophisticated wine is highly sought after by those who have discovered the wonders of New Zealand Pinot Noir.
#10952
Original Price: $41.99
Sale Price: $35.69

3. Olssens Pinot Noir "Jackson Berry" - 2006
The wines from Olssens of Bannockburn are always sophisticated and elegant. Their flagship wine is this intense Pinot Noir, which shows floral fruit with plum and black cherry nuances. It is drinking very well today, but could easily be held for years to come.
#13113
Original Price: $39.99
Sale Price: $33.99

2. Wellies Sauvignon Blanc, Marlborough - 2007
The Wairau Valley in Marlborough is New Zealand's original and most famous area for growing excellent Sauvignon Blanc. The Wellies bottling is incredibly approachable, with a nose full of gooseberries, fresh herbs, and melon. An incredibly fresh, dry white wine to suit any number of occasions.
#20366
Original Price: $14.99
Sale Price: $9.99

1. Ata Rangi Pinot Noir, Martinborough - 2006
This delicious red wine comes from the region of Martinborough, at the southern tip of the North Island of New Zealand. The producers of this Pinot Noir were highly concerned with preserving varietal typicity, and the wine exhibits beautiful redcurrant and red cherry fruit flavors.
#13130
Original Price: $41.99
Sale Price: $35.69
