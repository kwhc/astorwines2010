Our Top 11
Ports and Madeiras
(...our lists go to 11)


11. Quinta do Infantado Tawny Port - NV (750)
Tawny port just doesn't get better than this one from Quinta do Infantado. Excellent body, with rich notes of roasted hazelnuts and sultanas followed by just a hint of sweetness. Simply delicious.
#50504
Original Price: $14.99
Sale Price: $12.74

10. Quinta do Infantado, White Port - NV (750)
White port is a more rare type of this fortified wine. In fact, the color is more of an amber color. This is still showing lots of nut-driven character that could easily remind one of a lighter-styled madeira. The fruit is fresh too and full of citrus and almonds. This is on the dry side, but still has a touch of residual sugar.
#12426
Original Price: $16.99
Sale Price: $14.44

9. Colheita Madeira, Broadbent - 1996 (750)
Smooth with a nice, full texture and nutty finish. "Colheita" ports and madeiras are required to spend 7 and 5 years respectively in oak before release. This example is pefect as a dessert with Manchego and quince paste.
#21233
Original Price: $41.99
Sale Price: $35.69

8. Dow's LBV Port - 2003 (750)
An LBV (Late Bottled Vintage) port is different than a vintage port that's bottled two years after harvest. Bottled anywhere from four to six years after harvest, LBVs have been aged longer in cask and are therefore more approachable upon release.
#20651
Original Price: $19.99
Sale Price: $16.99

7. Justino's Rainwater, Madeira (750)
This style of madeira is so named because it is said to be soft as rainwater and the "color of straw, glistening after a shower of rain." Medium-dry, it is perfect with pâtés and cheeses or as an after-dinner sipper.
#57804
Original Price: $12.99
Sale Price: $11.04

6. Graham's Vintage Port - 2003 (375)
The Graham's port house is probably one of the most recognizable names in the port world. Their style of port is ultra-rich with candied aromas of freshly macerated black berries. The fruit, sugar and tannin levels are all elevated, and even more so in this vintage.
#14481
Original Price: $49.99
Sale Price: $42.49

5. Broadbent Madeira 10 Yr. Malmsey - NV (750)
This dark, amber, coffee-scented madeira will bring a smile to your face after a meal. With tangy dark fruits and plenty of woodsy character, it shows all the caramel complexity we like in these special fortified wines.
#21232
Original Price: $45.99
Sale Price: $39.09

4. Charleston Sercial Madeira, The Rare Wine Company - NV (750)
Charleston Sercial celebrates the refined, delicate Madeiras that the 18th- and 19th-century Charlestonians prized. Like any great Sercial, this miraculously balances power and delicacy. With its nutty, tangy flavors, it is not only a wonderful aperitif, it's great with many foods.
#12277
Original Price: $53.99
Sale Price: $45.89

3. Graham's Six Grapes Port - NV (750)
Sweet and fruity, this dark ruby vintage-character port is a robust wine with a thick, viscous texture. Earthy, flowery notes show on the nose and there is plenty of super-ripe, jammy blackberry fruit in the mouth.
#57614
Original Price: $19.99
Sale Price: $16.99

2. Fonseca Vintage Porto - 2000 (750)
The Fonseca port house was founded in the early 1800s. This vintage port is incredibly approachable. The fruit is dark, ripe and full of mullberries. The tannins are surprisingly accessible today.
#56106
Original Price: $94.99
Sale Price: $80.74

Elegant and rich, this dark, juicy Port has excellent balance of fruit, acid, sugar, and tannin. Not too sweet, not too big, it is easy to drink and enjoy.
