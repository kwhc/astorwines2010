Astor's Top German Wines
(our lists go up to 11)


11. Bernkastel Kurfurstlay Riesling, J&H Selbach - 2007 (750) -- A touch sweet
When one can drink such a delightfully lively and fruity white for this price, it's a wonder that anyone pays more for mediocre Chardonnay. Those in the know, however, will reap the benefits both at the table and the cash register.
#42724
Original Price: $10.99
Sale Price: $9.34

10. Rüdesheimer Klosterlay Riesling Kabinett, Leitz - 2007 (750) -- Lightly sweet
This Riesling is chiseled just right with minerals, mint leaf, and peaches on the palate. Tangy and just delightfully fresh.
#48889
Original Price: $17.99
Sale Price: $15.29

9. Gewürztraminer Spätlese, Pfeffingen - 2006 (750) -- Off-dry
If you like Gewürztraminer but are occasionally put off by its exuberant armonatics, this delicate, reticent style will be perfect. Unmistakably floral yet reserved and pretty, this slightly sweetwine is an excellent choice for exotic, highly spiced southeast Asian fare.
#18650
Original Price: $29.99
Sale Price: $25.49

8. Nahe Riesling "Monziger Halenberg" Spätlese, Schäfer-Fröhlich -- 2006 (750) -- Off-dry
A plump Riesling with great balance and yellow stone fruit aromas: yellow plums, yellow peaches, and a touch of Meyer lemon. Finishes more off-dry than bone-dry. Drink chilled.
#19971
Original Price: $37.99
Sale Price: $32.29

7. Diel de Diel, Schlossgut Diel - 2007 (750) -- Dry
"Imagine if Bob Parker owned one of the 1st-Growth Bordeaux." -Terry Theise. Armin Diel is Germany's preeminent wine writer; his clout as a pundit is only surpassed by his skill as a vigneron. This is an oddball blend of Pinot Gris, Chardonnay, Riesling, and Pinot Blanc that sees some time in barrel.
#21042
Original Price: $24.99
Sale Price: $21.24

6. Riesling Spätlese "Meddersheimer Rheingrafenberg", Hexamer - 2007 (750) -- Off-dry
Quartz is to the Nahe what slate is to the Mosel. Enjoy this beautifully peachy, minerally, ripe bottle on its own or with spicy southeast Asian fare.
#21041
Original Price: $32.99
Sale Price: $28.04

5. Weilberg Riesling, Grosses Gewächs, Pfeffingen - 2006 (750) -- Dry
A dry yet very well-balanced Riesling with great aromas of ripe pink grapefruit and white flowers. Drink with just a light chill and enjoy alongside seafood.
#19841
Original Price: $38.99
Sale Price: $33.14

4. Goldberg Riesling Trocken, Van Volxem - 2007 (750) -- Dry
These 30-year-old ungrafted vines grown on red slate produce Riesling of extraordinary complexity, showing pink grapefruit and intense mineral character. The estate emulates a dry style of wine that was popular in Germany in the 1800s.
#20755
Original Price: $34.99
Sale Price: $29.74

3. Iphöfer Kronsberg Riesling Spätlese Trocken, Wirsching - 2007 (750) -- Dry
You can't go wrong with the German wines from the Wirsching estate. This is truly a bottle that will make you drool with happiness.
#18629
Original Price: $42.99
Sale Price: $36.54

2. Pündericher Marienburg Riesling Spätlese Trocken ***, Clemens Busch - 2005 (750) -- Dry yet quite full-bodied
Clemens Busch is making terroir-driven, biodynamic wines. The fruit here is very complex, earthy, full of white pears, yellow citrus fruits and quince. The wine is aged for an unusually long time (by German standards). Only bottled in 2007. You'll note the intense yellow color.
#19995
Original Price: $34.99
Sale Price: $29.74

1. Piesporter Falkenberg Riesling, Reuscher-Haart - 2007 (LTR) -- Lightly sweet
This estate makes an awesomely voluptuous and sumptuous style of Riesling, even in this liter bottle, which is full of fleshy mango, peach, and honey-ripe, Piesporter perfection. The wine is rich and has only a hint of mouth-watering sweetness.
#21597
Original Price: $16.99
Sale Price: $14.44
